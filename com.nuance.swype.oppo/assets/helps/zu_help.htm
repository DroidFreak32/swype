  • Swype

    I-Swype yindlela esheshayo ukufaka umbhalo. Ikuvumela ukufaka igama ngokudweba phakathi nezinhlamvu. Beka umunwe wakho phezu kwenhlamvu yokuqala yegama bese udweba indlela ukusuka kwinhlamvu nganye ukuya kwenye, uphakamise ngemuva kwenhlamvu yokugcina. I-Swype izofaka izikhala lapho kudingeka khona.

    Ungasebenzisa umunwe wakho ukudweba izinhlamvu namagama i-Swype izowashintshela embhalweni. Ungadweba izinhlamvu ukusuka-kwesobunxele-ukuya kwesokudla noma phezu kwenhlamvu ngayinye. Cindezela u-ABC/123 ukushintshashintsha phakathi kwesimo senhlamvu nophawu.

      3. Thinta ukhiye wesikhala phakathi kwegama ngalinye

      2. Ngemuva kokufaka izimpawu,hambisa iminwe emibili ukuya phezulu phezu kwendawo yokubhalela
      3. Isimo sombhalo wesandla sizoqonda ukunqathuzisa okusebenzisa ukuphathwa kaningi-ningi bese kubhala izinhlamvu ngoosonhlamvukazi
