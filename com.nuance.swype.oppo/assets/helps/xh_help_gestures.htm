• Izijekuzisi ze-Swype

    Ukujekuzisa nge-Swype yindlela emfutshane phezu kwekhibhodi ukugqiba imisebenzi eqhelekileyo ngokukhawuleza.


    Eli lungu alifumaneki xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.


    • Ukuya kwikhibhodi yokuhlela

      Ukuya kwikhibhodi yokuHlela, Swype ukusuka ukuya kwisitshixo seeMpawu (?123) phezu kwekhibhodi.

    • Ukuya kwikhibhodi yamanani

      Ukufika kwibhodi yamaNani ngokukhawuleza, Swype ukusuka ukuya kwinani 5.

    • Ukufihla ikhibhodi

      Ukufihla ikhibhodi ngokulula, Swype nje ukususela kwisitshixo se-Swype ukuya kwisitshixo wokuBuyisela emva.

    • Cima ukwenza isithuba esizenzekelayo

      Nqanda ukuvuleka kwesithuba ngokuzenzekela ngaphambi kwegama elilandelayo ngokuSwype ukususela kwisitshixo sesiThuba ukuya kwisitshixo sokuBuyisela emva.

    • Ukuhlela ubukhulu begama

      Tshintsha ubukhulu bonobumba wegama ngemva kokumfaka ngokuthayipha igama uze uswayiphe ukusuka ku ukuya kwisitshixo seShift . I-Word Choice List enelungelo lokukhetha oonobumba abakhulu izakubonakala, ikuvumela ukuba ukhethe oonobumba abancinane, ooNobumba abakhulu, okanye ALL CAPS.

    • Iimpawu zokubhala

      Indlela elula yokufaka iimpawu zokuloba kuku-Swype ukusuka kuphawu lombuzo, ikhoma, ungxi, okanye olunye uphawu lokubhala ukuya kwisitshixo sesithuba endaweni yokuyicinezela.
