Nge-Swype, unobuchule bokutshintsha ngendlela engabonakalisi phakathi kwezimo ezine zeminikelo ezahlukileyyo – Uku-Swype, uku-Thetha, uku-Bhala okanye uku-Thayipha.

  • Swype

    I-Swype yindlela ekhawulezayo yokufaka umbhalo. Ikuvumela ukufaka igama ngokuzoba phakathi koonobumba. Beka Umnwe wakho phezu konobumba wokuqala wegama uze uzobe indlela ukusuka kunobumba ngamnye ukuya komnye, uphakamise emva konobumba wokugqibela. I-Swype izofaka izithuba apho kuyimfuneko khona.

    Funda ngokongezelelweyo
    • Isitshixo seSwype

      Isitshixo seSwype sisitshixo esinophawu lweSwype kuso. Cinezela ubambe isitshixo seSwype ukufumana iiSethingi zeSwype.

      Isitshixo seSwype siyasetyenziswa kwakhona ukuqala imijekulo eminingi yeSwype.

    • Izijekuzisi ze-Swype

      Ukujekuzisa nge-Swype yindlela emfutshane phezu kwekhibhodi ukugqiba imisebenzi eqhelekileyo ngokukhawuleza. Eli lungu alifumaneki xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.

      • Ukuya kwikhibhodi yokuhlela Ukuya kwikhibhodi yokuHlela, Swype ukusuka ukuya kwisitshixo seeMpawu (?123) phezu kwekhibhodi.
      • Ukuya kwikhibhodi yamanani Ukufika kwibhodi yamaNani ngokukhawuleza, Swype ukusuka ukuya kwinani 5.
      • Ukufihla ikhibhodi Ukufihla ikhibhodi ngokulula, Swype nje ukususela kwisitshixo se-Swype ukuya kwisitshixo wokuBuyisela emva.
      • Cima ukwenza isithuba esizenzekelayo Nqanda ukuvuleka kwesithuba ngokuzenzekela ngaphambi kwegama elilandelayo ngokuSwype ukususela kwisitshixo sesiThuba ukuya kwisitshixo sokuBuyisela emva.
      • Ukuhlela ubukhulu begama Tshintsha ubukhulu bonobumba wegama ngemva kokumfaka ngokuthayipha igama uze uswayiphe ukusuka ku ukuya kwisitshixo seShift . I-Word Choice List enelungelo lokukhetha oonobumba abakhulu izakubonakala, ikuvumela ukuba ukhethe oonobumba abancinane, ooNobumba abakhulu, okanye ALL CAPS.
      • Iimpawu zokubhala Indlela elula yokufaka iimpawu zokuloba kuku-Swype ukusuka kuphawu lombuzo, ikhoma, ungxi, okanye olunye uphawu lokubhala ukuya kwisitshixo sesithuba endaweni yokuyicinezela.
      • Khetha Konke, Sika, Kopa kwaye uNcamathisele Khetha Konke: Swype ukusuka ukuya ‘a’
        Sika: Swype ukusuka ukuya ‘x’
        Kopa: Swype ukusuka ukuya ‘c’
        Ncamathisela: Swype ukusuka ukuya ‘v’
      • Iindlela zokusebenza ezimfutshaneIimaphu zikaGoogle: Swype ukusuka ukuya ‘g’, kulandele ‘m’
      • FunaBhala ngokucacileyo omnye umbhalo uze uSwype ukusuka ku- ukuya ku-S ukwenza uphando olukhawulezayo kwiwebhusayithi.
      • Ukushintshela kulwimi lokugqibela olusetyenzisiweXa usebenzisa iilwimi ezininzininzi, indlela ekhawulezayo yokubuyela emva kulwimi olungaphambili kukuSwype ukusuka ku ukuya kwisitshixo seSpace.
    • Ukufakela oonobumba abangababini

      Ukwenza ngcono ukucoseleka xa ufakela oonobumba abangababini, bhala kancinane okanye wenze irhintyela phezu konobumba. Umzekelo, ukufumana u-“mm” ku- “mmandla”, bhala kancinane phezu kwesitshixo sika- “m”. Eli lungu alifumaneki xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.

    • Ukukhetha igama

      Ukwamkela igama elize ngemposiso eliphakanyiswayo kwi- Word Choice List, qhubeka njalo uSwayiphe. Okanye, hambisa uluhlu ngokutsala umnwe wakho, uze ukhethe igama olifunayo. Eli lungu alifumaneki xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.

      Ukukhetha umbhalo kuluhlu, hambisa umnwe wakho ngesangqa ukuze uzwe uluhlu lweenkcukacha. Ukuhambisela kwesokunene kuya phambili kuluhlu; ukuhambisela kwesokhohlo kuya emva kuluhlu. Ukuphakamisa umnwe kunikela ngombhalo wokugqibela woluhlu obuthethiwe. Ukukhetha igama kwi-Word Choice List, hambisa umnwe uye phezulu kwikhibhodi kude kuvakale igama lokuqala kuluhlu, uze uqalise ukushukuma okusisangqa. Eli lungu alifumaneki kuphela xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.

    • Ukubhala amagama ngooNobumba abakhulu

      Eli lungu alifumaneki xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.

      Bhala igama ngooNobumba abakhulu ngokuSwype umnwe wakho ukusuka kunobumba wokuqala ukuya phezulu ukudlula phezulu kwikhibhodi uze, ungaphakamsanga, uhambise umnwe uwehlise kwakhona ukuya kunobumba olandelayo.

      Tshintsha ubukhulu bonobumba wegama ngemva kokumfaka ngokuthayipha igama uze uswayiphe ukusuka ku ukuya kwisitshixo seShift . I-Word Choice List enelungelo lokukhetha oonobumba abakhulu izakubonakala, ikuvumela ukuba ukhethe oonobumba abancinane, ooNobumba abakhulu, okanye ALL CAPS.

      Ukufaka isimo seCAPS Lock, thayipha nje kabini phezu kwesitshixo se-Shift .

    • Ukuvula isithuba ngokuzenzekela

      I-Swype ngokuzenzekela ifakela isithuba phakathi kwamagama xa uSwype igama elilandelayo kwisivakalisi. Ungacima okanye uvule isimo sokuvula isithuba ngokuzenzekela kwiiSethingi ze-Swype.

      Ukuvula isithuba ngokuzenzekela kungacinywa kwelinye igama ngokuSwype ukusuka kwisitshixo sesiThuba ukuya kwisitshixo se-Backspace.

      Eli lungu alifumaneki xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.

    • Ukufaka igama endaweni yelinye

      Faka igama endaweni yelinye ngokuthayipha phezu kwalo, uze ukhethe igama olifunayo kwi-Word Choice List, okanye ubhale nje ngokucacileyo igama elo uze uSwype igama elitsha. Igama elitsha lizongena endaweni yelingafanele /yelingalungile

      Igama lingabhalwa ngokucacileyo ngokuthayipha igama elo uze ubethe okanye kuthayitshwe igama kabini.

    • Ukuxhumaxhuma phakathi kwamagama

      Ngelinye ixesha ukuphepha oonobumba xa uSwype kuqinisekisa ukuba ufumana igama olifunayo ngelixa lokuqala nje.

      Umzekelo, “ezona” no “ezina” zingazotywa ngendlela efanayo – kodwa lumka, akuyomfuneko ukushukuma usuka komnye unobumba usiya komnye ngomgca othe ngqo. Ukuphepha u-“o” xa uswype umnwe wakho ku”n” kuqinisekisa ukuba “ezona” isekuqaleni kuluhlu lwe-Word Choice.

    • Balingisi enye

      Cinezela ubambe isitshixo ukukhupha uluhlu lweempawu zesitshixo eso, ezifana neempawu ezifana no - @ no- %, namanani.

      Thayipha isitshixo seZimpawu (?123) ezizakusiwa kwikhibhodi yeeMpawu.

      Qaphela ukuthi ZONKE iimpawu ziphumeleliswa yi-Swype ukusuka kwikhibhodi enkulu (okanye ukwazi ukuzibona okanye hayi). UngaSwype usebenzisa oku okubonakala kwikhibhodi, kodwa uzakufumana kuphela amagama anobuncinane benani elinye okanye uphawu.

    • Ukwengeza nokucima amagama

      I-Swype yongeza ngobukrelekrele nawaphi amagama amatsha owasebenzisa kwisiChazimazwi sakho Buqu.

      Ungongezelela futhi igama ngokulibhala ngokucacileyo uze uthayiphe . Thayipha isikhokhelo esivelayo ukongeza igama.

      Ukucima igama,cinezela ubambe igama kwi-Word Choice List, uze uthayiphe KULUNGILE kwingxoxo yokuqinisekisa. Eli lungu alifumaneki xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.

  • Thetha

    Ungathetha ukungenisa okuphakathi embhalweni kuko konke kususela kumbhalo nemiyalezo ye-imeyile ukuya kwi-Facebook nohlaziyo lwe-Twitter.

    Funda ngokongezelelweyo
    • Iimpawu zokubhala

      Akukho mfuneko yokongezelela iimpawu zokubhala ngesandla. Yitsho nje uphawu lokubhala olufunayo uze uqhubeke. Zama oku:

      1. Cinezela isitshixo sezwi uze uqale ukuthetha.
      2. Okutshoyo: Idina ibimnandi kakhulu uphawu lwesikhuzo
      3. Okufumanayo: Idina ibimnandi kakhulu!

    • Umnikelo weliZwi awufumaneki kwezinyenye iikhibhodi

  • Bhala

    Ungasebenzisa umnwe wakho ukuzoba oonobumba namagama i-Swype izowatshintshela embhalweni. Ungazoba oonobumba ukusuka-kwesokhohlo-ukuya kwesokunene okanye phezu konobumba ngamnye. Cinezela u-ABC/123 ukutshintshatshintsha phakathi kwesimo sonobumba nophawu.

    Funda ngokongezelelweyo
    • Phumel. umbhalo wes.
      1. Cinezela ubambe i- uhambise umnwe wakho ukuya kwisitshixo sokubhala ngesandla.
      2. Zoba oonobumba ngomnwe phezu kwendawo yokubhalela ngesandla.
      3. Thayipha isitshixo sesithuba phakathi kwegama ngalinye
    • Ukujekuzisa ngokuPhatha kaNinginingi

      Ukujekuzisa ngokuphatha kaninginingi kukuvumela ukuba ufeze imisebenzi elula, efana nokubhala amagama okanye oonobumba ngoonobumba abakhulu.

      1. Zoba oonobumba abancane kwiphedi yokuzobela
      2. Emva kokufaka iimpawu,hambisa iminwe emibini ukuya phezulu phezu kwendawo yokubhalela
      3. Isimo sokubhala sizakuqonda ukujekuzisa okusebenzisa ukuphathwa kaningi-ningi size sibhale oonobumba ngoonobumba abakhulu

    • Umbhalo wesandla awufumaneki kwezinye iikhibhodi.

      Eli lungu alifumaneki xa iinkonzo zokuba nokufikelwa kwisixokelelwano( system accessibility service) zo kuBona-ngo-kuBamba zilayitiwe.

  • Cofa

    Umnikelo wohlobo lwakudala lwekhibhodi esebenza ngezandla. Ukuthayipha umnikelo phezu kwekhibhodi ye-Swype kwenziwa lula nokuba nobuchule zizimo ezincedisayo:

    Funda ngokongezelelweyo
    • Ukulungisa ukuThayipha okwenziwe ngobuxelegu

      Akuyomfuneko ukuthayipha unobumba ngamnye kakuhle. Zama kakuhle kakhulu yaye i-Swype izonikela ngeengcebiso zamagama ngobukrelekrele obukhulu.

    • UkuGqitywa kweGama

      I-Swype iyakwazi ukuthelekelela igama lakho xa uthayipha kuphela oonobumba abambalwa.

  • Iilwimi

    Ukutshintsha iilwimi kwikhibhodi: Cinezela ubambe isitshixo sesithuba. Khetha ulwimi olufunayo ukusuka kwimenyu evelayo.

  • Swype Connect

    I-Swype Connect isivumela ukuhambisa izihlaziyi nokusebenza okunamandla kwisixhobo sakho ngqo! Njengoba i-Swype Connect ikwazi ukusebenza kwi 3G, sisoloko njalo nje sicebisa ukufumana ukunxibelelana nge-WiFi

    Funda ngokongezelelweyo
    • Ukulayisha phantsi iilwimi

      Ukongeza iilwimi ezongezelelweyo ku-Swype kulula:

      1. Cinezela futhi ubambe u- uze ukhethe iiLwimi.
      2. Ukusuka kwimenyu yeeLwimi, khetha Layisha phantsi iilwimi
      3. Cinezela phezu kolwimi ukulayisha phantsi kwakho kuza kuqala ngokuzenzekela.

    • I-Swype Connect ayifumaneki kuzo zonke iikhibhodi.
