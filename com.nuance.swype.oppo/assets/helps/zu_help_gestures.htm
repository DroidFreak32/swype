• Izinqathuzisi ze-Swype

    Ukunqathuzisa nge-Swype yizindlela ezinqamulayo phezu kwekhibhodi ukufeza imisebenzi yenjwayelo ngokushesha.


    Lesi simo asitholakali uma inkonzo yohlelo i-Explore-by-Touch isebenza


    • Ukuya kwikhibhodi yokulungisa

      Ukuya kwikhibhodi yokulungisa, yithi Swype ukusuka ukuya kukhiye wezimpawu (?123) phezu kwekhibhodi.

    • Ukuya kwikhibhodi yamanani

      Ukufika kwikhibhodi yamaNani ngokushesha, yithi Swype ukusuka ukuya kwinani 5.

    • Ukufihla ikhibhodi

      Ukufihla ikhibhodi kalula, kufanele uthi Swype kusukela kukhiye we-Swype ukuya kukhiye wokubuyela emuva.

    • Cima ukwenza izikhalaokuzenzekelayo

      Vimbela ukuvuleka kwesikhala ngokuzenzekela ngaphambi kwegama elilandelayo ngokuthi Swype ukususela kukhiye wesiKhala ukuya kukhiye wokubuyela emuva.

    • Ukulungisa ubukhulu begama

      Shintsha ubukhulu benhlamvu yegama ngemuva kokuyifaka ngokuthinta igama bese uthi Swype ukusuka ku ukuya kukhiye weShift . I-Word Choice List enokhetho losonhlamvukazi izobonakala, ikuvumela ukuba ukhethe izinhlamvu ezincane, oSonhlamvukazi, noma ALL CAPS.

    • Uphawu lokuloba

      Indlela elula yokufaka izimpawu zokuloba ukuthi Swype kususela kuphawu lombuzo, ikhoma, ungqi, noma olunye uphawu lokuloba ukuya kukhiye weSikhala endaweni yokuthinta.
